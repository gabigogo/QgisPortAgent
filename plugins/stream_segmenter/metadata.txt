about=Given a stream centerline layer (LineString or MultiLineString), this plugin divides each feature into equal-length intervals of a user-specified distance (default 1 mile) measured outward from the downstream endpoint. Each output segment receives three new attribute fields: a segment number (integer, 1 = most downstream), a human-readable range label (e.g. "0-1 mi"), and a unique stream–segment identifier (e.g. "Bear_Creek_mile_01"). Supports miles or kilometres, optional preservation of source attributes, and processing of selected features only.
